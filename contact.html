Volunteer With Us!

What is The Mississauga Chess Club

The Mississauga Chess Club is the largest chess club in Canada that operates on two nights, one for youth and one for adults and advanced youths. Tournament games are played weekly.

What Our Volunteers do

Our volunteers help us do many of the tasks that make a chess club possible. Kids look forward to playing chess each week, and volunteers help make that possible. Duties include setting up and putting away chess club equipment, assisting kids and parents, playing and helping kids new to chess. This is a great opportunity especially if chess is an interest.

Getting Started

  1. We will give hours to high school students completing their 40 hours.
  2. Knowledge of how chess works is not a requirement and we can teach if you are new!
  3. A Resume is Not Required
